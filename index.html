Intro Tomocoin.

We are building a new social network from ground up with a built-in cryptocurrency. Our goal is to make Tomo Network the host of multiple successful decentralized applications, and Tomocoin one of the most valuable and important digital assets for millions of people. Our vision is our project is worth billions next five years.
Download White paper

Why TOMOCOIN.

- Content contributor's network: The network is created by and for contributors. Help solving other's problems; create and publish useful contents and receive rewards from the network.

- Built-in micro payment with a store of value characteristics: unique architecture allowing for an easy monetization with micropayment in publishing, gaming, crowdfunding etc. but also a secure store of value of coin on a public blockchain.


- First rate usability using technology such as mobile first, AI, AR, location and camera based services.

- User privacy: no tracking, no data mining and surveillance; allowing for anonymous identity and content creation.

Wallet

A secure and user-friendly coin wallet that gives financial control back to the end-users is one of the most important parts of the infrastructure of the “Internet of Value”. It will bridge the gap between a cryptocurrency enthusiast and a mainstream user – and possibly increase the adoption rate of cryptocurrency by 10-fold if done right. We want to invest in building such a wallet, firstly to host Tomocoin and Ethereum and later multiple other cryptographic tokens and currencies.
To safely store Tomocoin, Ethereum, and other coins and tokens
A Tomo debit card will be linked with Tomo wallet for payments in physical locations
Users will be able to safely send Tomocoin to friends and contacts

Debit Card

Connect your Tomo account wallet to your Tomo debit card to spend your coins as fiat currency anywhere in the world. Fees will be minimal and as the card uses Visa infrastructure you can use it almost anywhere in the world.
